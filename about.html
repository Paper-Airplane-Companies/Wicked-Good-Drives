About Wicked Good Drives

We bring a premium golf simulator experience directly to your event.

Wicked Good Drives was built to give families, businesses, venues, and event hosts across Alachua County a more interactive, flexible, and memorable way to entertain their guests. We deliver a polished mobile golf simulator setup that works for private parties, corporate events, fundraisers, weddings, breweries, tailgates, and community events.

Our story

A local business built around quality, innovation, and fun.

Wicked Good Drives was created with one big goal in mind: make event entertainment more exciting, more convenient, and more memorable. Instead of sending guests off to a golf course, we bring the golf experience right to the event with a setup that feels polished, approachable, and built for a great time.

We love creating experiences that people talk about long after the event ends. Whether the crowd is full of experienced golfers, first-timers, coworkers, families, or wedding guests, the simulator gives everyone a chance to jump in, compete, laugh, and have fun.

Our mission

Make great events even better with mobile entertainment that stands out.

Our mission is simple: bring a premium golf simulator rental experience to events across Gainesville, High Springs, Newberry, Jonesville, and the surrounding Alachua County area in a way that is easy for hosts and exciting for guests.

We believe great entertainment should be flexible, all-weather friendly, beginner-friendly, and professional enough for high-end events while still being fun for everyone in attendance.
