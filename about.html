About Wicked Good Drives

We bring premium golf simulator entertainment directly to the people, places, and events that matter most.

Wicked Good Drives was built to create a more exciting, more flexible, and more memorable event experience across Alachua County. Instead of sending guests somewhere else, we bring the golf experience straight to your event with a polished mobile setup that feels premium, approachable, and built for fun.

Our story

Built around quality, innovation, and unforgettable event energy.

Wicked Good Drives was created for hosts who want more than standard entertainment. We wanted to build something that feels interactive, premium, and easy to bring to private parties, corporate events, weddings, fundraisers, breweries, and community gatherings.

The result is a mobile golf simulator experience that creates conversation, competition, and excitement the moment guests step up to play. It is fun for serious golfers, welcoming for beginners, and flexible enough to work in a wide range of event settings.
